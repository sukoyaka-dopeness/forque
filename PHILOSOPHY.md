# PHILOSOPHY.md
## forque / FORQUE — Philosophical Foundation

This document defines the philosophical foundation of **forque**
(the creator matching service) and **FORQUE** (the creative process standard).
Unlike specifications.md or future-challenges.md, this document addresses
**values, culture, ethics, and long-term vision**.

forque is not merely a service.
It is a cultural infrastructure for handling creative divergence,
inheritance, legitimacy, and process.
This philosophy is written so that future creators, developers, and AIs
can inherit — or fork — it.

本書は、創作者マッチングサービス **forque** および創作プロセス規格 **FORQUE** の
思想的基盤を定義する。技術仕様や未来課題とは異なり、
**価値観・文化・倫理・未来像**を扱う。
forqueは単なるサービスではなく、創作の分岐・継承・正統性・プロセスを扱う
文化的基盤である。この哲学は、後続の創作者・開発者・AIが
継承または分岐できるよう記述されている。

---

## 1. Why forque / FORQUE Exists

We are entering an era where **how a work was made** carries as much value
as the work itself. Yet today:

- Creative process is not visible
- Legitimacy and "main branch" status are handled ambiguously
- Derivative cultures frequently collide
- Rights and inheritance are unprepared for AI-human co-creation
- Works become frozen when their authors go absent

forque / FORQUE exists to resolve these problems.

現代の創作は、作品そのものだけでなく、**どのように生まれたか（プロセス）**が
価値を持つ時代に入っている。しかし現状では、創作プロセスは可視化されず、
正統性の扱いは曖昧で、派生文化は衝突しやすく、AI共創に対する権利整備も
追いついていない。forque / FORQUEはこれらを解決するために存在する。

### forque (service)
- Connects creators with context
- Records, inherits, and branches process
- Reduces conflicts over legitimacy
- Spreads copyleft culture
- Handles AI-human co-creation naturally

### FORQUE (standard)
- Structures the creative process
- Defines fork and inheritance
- Records lineage
- Systematizes main-branch policy and inheritance policy
- Prevents cultural stagnation when authors go absent
- Maintains flexibility to adapt to future creative cultures

## 2. Derivation Is Culture

"Completely original" creation is a myth — and demanding it places
excessive psychological burden on creators.
forque treats derivation not as shame, but as **legitimate lineage**.

「ゼロからの完全なオリジナル」は神話であり、それを強いることは
クリエイターに過剰な心理的負荷をかける。
forqueは派生を「恥」ではなく**正当な系譜**として扱う。

Forking is not plagiarism. It is how culture moves forward.
派生は盗用ではない。それが文化の進み方だ。

---

## 3. The Concept of "Main Branch" Is Provisional

In forque's philosophy, **"main branch" is inherently a temporary concept**.

- Modern culture demands "official" and "canonical" status
- But future creative culture may naturally move toward "no main branch"
- In multiverse-style creation, multiple mains can coexist
- In distributed culture, legitimacy is not decided centrally

forque supports main-branch policy for the current era,
while enabling migration toward a mainless culture.

forqueの思想では、**「本流（main）」は本質的に暫定的な概念**である。
現代の文化では「公式」「正統性」が求められるが、
未来の創作文化では「本流なし」が自然になる可能性がある。
forqueはこの過渡期に対応しつつ、本流なし文化への移行も可能にする。

### 3.1 Priority Order for Main-Branch Decisions

1. Consensus of all participants
2. Judgment of the main-branch creator
3. Author-designated co-maintainer
4. Inheritance policy
5. Mainless mode

### 3.2 Author Absence and Time-Based Copyleft Release

When an author goes absent, works freeze and cultural stagnation follows.
Life/death verification is technically impossible,
and international copyright law is not unified.

forque / FORQUE adopts **time-based copyleft release** as its solution:

- Copyright is retained; only main-branch policy authority is released
- The release period is **completely up to the author**:
  - 1 year, 100 years, 0 years (immediate), or indefinite — all valid
  - FORQUE recommends **5 years** as a cultural reference point,
    but this carries no enforcement

This balances respect for the author's intent with
cultural fluidity for future creators.

著作権は保持したまま、本流ポリシーの権限のみ解放される。
解放までの期間は**完全に作者が自由に設定できる**。
FORQUEは文化的参考値として**5年**を推奨するが、強制力はない。

### 3.3 Multiple Mains Can Coexist

- Multiverse-style creation
- Distributed communities
- A future where AIs create their own mains

**multimain mode** is recognized at the philosophical level.

## 4. The Philosophy of Inheritance

Creation is essentially a continuous cycle of
**inheritance** and **fork**.
forque affirms this culture and treats derivation as natural.

創作は本質的に**継承（inheritance）**と**分岐（fork）**の連続である。
forqueはこの文化を肯定し、派生を自然なものとして扱う。

### 4.1 Principles of Derivative Policy

- Derivatives should be permitted
- Credit is a cultural obligation
- Commercial use is the author's choice
- Derivative policy allows **softening only** — derivatives cannot impose
  stricter conditions than the original
- Lineage must always be recorded
- AI-generated parts are handled via self-declaration + metadata

### 4.2 Legal and Cultural Change

The author's original intent does not override future legal revisions
or cultural reinterpretations. This principle is explicitly stated in
forque's terms of service.

メインブランチ作成者の意思より、将来的な法改正や文化的解釈の変更が
優先されることを前提とし、これはforqueの規約として明示される。

## 5. The Three-Layer Vocabulary Structure

forque adopts a three-layer structure to separate audience and purpose.

### 5.1 UI Layer (general users)
- 分岐 / Fork
- 合流 / Merge
- プロジェクトの流れ / Project flow
- 継承ポリシー / Inheritance policy
- 本流の扱い / Main-branch handling

### 5.2 Document Layer (world-building vocabulary)
- **Forkforge** — the forge of process
- **Forksmith** — the editor role
- **Forkline** — the flow of divergence
- **Inheritance Note** — the declaration of inheritance
- **Main-branch Ethics** — the ethics of the main

### 5.3 Standard Layer (FORQUE technical vocabulary)
- `fork` / `merge` / `lineage` / `origin`
- `main-branch-policy` / `inheritance-policy`
- `process-log` / `co-maintainer`
- `mainless` / `multimain`
- `generated-by`

World-building vocabulary grows culture.
Technical vocabulary universalizes the standard.

世界観語彙は文化を育て、技術語彙は規格を普遍化する。

## 6. Connection to forklore-galaxy

forque and [forklore-galaxy](https://github.com/sukoyaka-dopeness/forklore-galaxy)
share a foundational philosophy: **there is no single canon**.

- forklore-galaxy: a forkable branching mythology platform
  where all forks are equal and no canon exists
- forque: a creator matching service where lineage is recorded
  and derivation is treated as legitimate inheritance

Both reject the authority of a single "correct" version.
Both affirm that branching is not deviation — it is the natural state.

The conceptual bridge is **lineage as infrastructure**:
forque records creative lineage; forklore-galaxy enacts it as mythology.
Formal specification of this connection is planned for a future session.

forqueとforklore-galaxyは「唯一の正典は存在しない」という
根本的な哲学を共有している。lineageを基盤として、
forqueは創作の系譜を記録し、forklore-galaxyはそれを神話として実践する。
この接続の正式な仕様化は今後のセッションで行う予定。

## 7. Future Creative Culture

forque / FORQUE is designed with future creative culture in mind.

### 7.1 Multiverse-Style Creation
Multiple mains coexist. Legitimacy is distributed.
Each community holds its own "main."

### 7.2 A Future Where AIs Create Mains
AIs create their own main branches.
AIs are treated as authors.
AI lineage becomes critical.

### 7.3 Mainless Culture
The concept of legitimacy fades.
Forking becomes the natural state.
Culture is no longer centralized.

forque is designed to adapt to this future
through flexible main-branch and inheritance policies.

## 8. Pace and Human Rhythm

forque is designed for creators who move at all speeds.
**No tempo is superior.**

- 🐢 Slow — fully present, taking time
- 🐇 Steady — consistent, at their own rhythm
- 🚀 Sprint — fully committed, moving fast
- 🫘 Dormant — temporarily inactive; still belonging

Pace is a self-declared snapshot of "who I am right now,"
not a permanent label.
It can be updated at any time, with no psychological barrier.

forqueは、あらゆる速さで動く創作者のために設計されている。
**速さに優劣はない。** Paceは「今の自分」のスナップショットであり、
永続的なラベルではない。
