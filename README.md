# forque

> A platform for creative lineage — where every work can become someone else's origin.

forque is a creator matching service built around the philosophy that
derivation is culture, not theft.
It records the creative process as **lineage**, making collaboration
visible, forkable, and permanent.

---

## What is forque?

- Match with creators by **Pace** (your creative tempo)
- Fork, inherit, and branch from existing works
- Record collaboration as **lineage** — not just output, but process
- Designed for humans who move slowly, quickly, or somewhere in between

## Design Principles

- Derivation is legitimate lineage, not plagiarism
- Pace is self-declared — no tempo is superior
- Process is as valuable as output
- The platform does not own your work
